Tema

What is a double negative?

A double negative happens when a sentence uses two negative words where only one is needed for a negative meaning.

Double negative

I don’t need no help.

Correct option 1

I don’t need any help.

Correct option 2

I need no help.

To correct it, remove one negative word or change one negative word into a positive word such as any, anybody, anything or anywhere.

Pattern 1

not / n’t + positive word

I can’t find anything.

Pattern 2

Affirmative verb + negative word

I can find nothing.

Pattern 3

Never + positive word

She never tells anyone.
